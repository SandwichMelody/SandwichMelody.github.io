Technology Unit Plan

Project: GSW 1110 Project 2: Literacy Narrative (for next time I teach GSW 1110)

Originally assigned as a standard alphabetic text project, this revised project would be assigned as a digital project requiring students to share their literacy narrative in one of three forms: audio, video, or documentary photo essay. This provides students with some level of choice but limits the need for extra assessment guidelines and allows for class time to realistically cover the use of various tools for each medium of writing. The project would be completed as Project 2 instead of Project 1, as I taught it most recently. The rhetoric unit would be shifted to the beginning of the course, allowing students to draw on lessons, in that unit, on audio and visual rhetoric. (I also think starting with rhetoric is a good approach for jumpstarting, if with a bit of intimidation, awareness and thinking about the options a writer has.)
